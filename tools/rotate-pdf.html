Understanding Rotation Angles

90¬∞ Right (Clockwise)

The most common rotation. Use this when pages need to be turned one quarter turn to the right. Ideal for portrait documents scanned in landscape mode.

180¬∞ (Upside Down)

Flips pages completely upside down. Perfect for documents scanned or photographed upside down.

90¬∞ Left (Counter-clockwise)

Rotates pages one quarter turn to the left. Use when 90¬∞ right isn't the correct direction or to undo a previous rotation.

Reset (0¬∞)

Returns pages to their original orientation, removing all previous rotations. Useful for undoing changes.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I rotate PDF pages online?

Upload your PDF file, select the rotation angle (90¬∞, 180¬∞, or 270¬∞), choose whether to rotate all pages or specific pages, and click Rotate PDF. Download your rotated file instantly.

Can I rotate only specific pages in a PDF?

Yes, you can choose to rotate all pages or specify a page range (e.g., 1-3, 5, 7-9) to rotate only selected pages while leaving others unchanged.

Is the PDF rotation permanent?

Yes, the rotation is saved in the PDF file. The rotated pages will display in the new orientation in any PDF viewer or when printed.

Does rotating PDFs reduce quality?

No, rotating PDFs is a lossless operation. The content, images, and text quality remain exactly the same, only the page orientation changes.

Is it safe to rotate PDFs online?

Yes, all processing happens locally in your browser. Files never upload to servers, ensuring complete privacy and security of your documents.

Can I rotate password-protected PDFs?

You can rotate password-protected PDFs if you have edit permissions. For encrypted PDFs, use our Unlock PDF tool first to remove protection, then rotate.

What if I rotate pages in the wrong direction?

Simply upload the file again and rotate in the opposite direction, or use the rotation angles to correct it (e.g., if you rotated 90¬∞ right but needed left, rotate 270¬∞ right).

Can I rotate PDFs on mobile devices?

Yes, our tool is fully mobile-responsive and works on smartphones and tablets. The interface adjusts for touch screens and smaller displays.
