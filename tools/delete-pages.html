Frequently Asked Questions

Is it really free to delete PDF pages?

Yes! JustPdf's PDF page deletion tool is completely free with no watermarks and no registration required. Delete pages from as many PDFs as you need, absolutely free. There are no file size limits or usage restrictions.

Is my PDF secure when deleting pages?

Absolutely! All PDF processing happens entirely in your browser using JavaScript. Your file never gets uploaded to our servers or any third-party service. Once you close your browser, all data is gone. This ensures complete privacy and security for sensitive documents.

Can I delete multiple pages at once?

Yes! You can delete multiple pages using comma-separated values (e.g., 1, 3, 5) or page ranges with hyphens (e.g., 1-5, 10-15). You can also combine both formats (e.g., 1, 3-5, 8, 10-12) to delete any combination of pages.

Will deleting pages affect my PDF quality?

No, deleting pages does not affect the quality of the remaining pages. All text, images, formatting, fonts, hyperlinks, and other elements are preserved exactly as they were in the original PDF.

What happens to page numbers after deletion?

After pages are deleted, the remaining pages are automatically renumbered sequentially starting from 1. For example, if you delete pages 1-3 from a 10-page PDF, the original page 4 becomes page 1, page 5 becomes page 2, and so on.

Can I delete all pages except certain ones?

While our tool requires you to specify which pages to delete, you can easily calculate which pages to remove. If you want to keep only pages 5-10 from a 20-page PDF, you would delete pages 1-4 and 11-20.

Is there a file size limit?

There's no strict file size limit, but very large files (over 100MB) may take longer to process depending on your device's performance. For best results with large files, use a modern browser and ensure sufficient RAM is available.

Do I need to install any software?

No software installation required! This is a web-based tool that works directly in your browser. Just visit our website, upload your PDF, and start deleting pages immediately. Works on Windows, Mac, Linux, iOS, and Android.
