Why Convert Word to PDF?

PDF is the universal standard for document sharing and distribution. Here's why converting Word to PDF is essential:

  • Universal compatibility: PDFs display identically on any device, operating system, or PDF reader
  • Preserve formatting: Lock in fonts, spacing, images, and layouts exactly as you designed them
  • Professional appearance: PDFs look polished and professional for business documents, resumes, and reports
  • Prevent editing: Recipients can view but not easily modify your document content
  • Smaller file sizes: PDFs are often smaller than Word documents, making them easier to email
  • Print-ready format: PDFs maintain exact dimensions and formatting for professional printing
  • Security options: PDFs can be password-protected and encrypted for sensitive documents

How do I convert Word to PDF online?

Is it safe to convert Word documents to PDF online?

Yes, absolutely. All conversion happens client-side in your browser using JavaScript libraries. Your Word files never leave your device and are not uploaded to any server. We cannot see, access, or store your documents in any way. 100% private.

Will my formatting be preserved?

Yes! Our converter preserves text, basic formatting (bold, italic, underline), images, tables, lists, and paragraph spacing. Very complex features like SmartArt, advanced charts, or custom fonts may render differently. For documents with simple to moderate formatting, results are excellent.

Why only DOCX files? What about DOC?

Our browser-based converter supports the modern DOCX format (.docx) which has been standard since Word 2007. Legacy .DOC files need to be converted to .DOCX first. Simply open your .DOC file in Microsoft Word and save it as .DOCX format, then use our converter.

While there's no strict limit, we recommend files under 25MB for best performance. Very large documents may take longer to process or could fail on devices with limited memory. If you have a large file, consider compressing images in Word first or splitting the document.

No! You don't need Microsoft Word or any other software installed. Our web-based converter works directly in your browser on any device - Windows, Mac, Linux, Chromebook, iOS, or Android. Just upload your .DOCX file and download the converted PDF.

Why choose PDF over Word format?

PDFs are ideal for final documents that shouldn't be edited: resumes, contracts, invoices, reports, brochures, and forms. They display identically everywhere, can't be easily modified, preserve your exact formatting, and are the standard for professional document distribution and archiving.

Common Use Cases

  • Resumes and CVs: Convert your resume to PDF before applying for jobs to ensure perfect formatting
  • Business reports: Share quarterly reports, presentations, and proposals in professional PDF format
  • Contracts and legal documents: Convert agreements to PDF to prevent unauthorized editing
  • Invoices and receipts: Create professional invoices in Word, convert to PDF for emailing
  • Academic papers: Submit essays, research papers, and dissertations in PDF format
  • Brochures and flyers: Design in Word, convert to PDF for printing or digital distribution

Tips for Best Results

  • Use standard fonts (Arial, Times New Roman, Calibri) for guaranteed compatibility
  • Embed all images directly in the Word document before conversion
  • Check your document in Print Preview mode before converting to see how it will look
  • Use Page Layout view in Word to verify margins and page breaks
  • Compress large images in Word before conversion to reduce final PDF file size
  • Avoid using advanced Word features like ActiveX controls or macros
  • For complex documents, save as PDF directly from Microsoft Word for best results
  • Always review the converted PDF before sharing to ensure everything looks correct
