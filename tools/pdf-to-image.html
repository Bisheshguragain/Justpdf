JPG vs PNG: Which Format to Choose?

JPG/JPEG Format

Best for: Photos, colorful graphics, complex images

Pros: Smaller file sizes, widely supported, good for web use

Cons: Lossy compression (some quality loss), no transparency support

PNG Format

Best for: Text documents, diagrams, logos, screenshots

Pros: Lossless compression (perfect quality), transparency support, sharp text

Cons: Larger file sizes, may be too large for web use without optimization

Understanding Quality Levels

  • Standard Quality (72 DPI): Best for web use, social media, and screen viewing. Creates smaller files that load quickly.
  • High Quality (150 DPI): Balanced quality suitable for most uses including presentations, documents, and moderate-resolution printing.
  • Maximum Quality (300 DPI): Professional print quality. Best for high-resolution printing, professional publications, and archiving. Creates larger files.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I convert PDF to image online?

Upload your PDF file, select the image format (JPG or PNG) and quality level, choose which pages to convert, and click Convert. Download your images instantly as individual files or in a ZIP archive.

What image formats are supported?

We support JPG/JPEG (best for photos and complex graphics) and PNG (best for text, diagrams, and images requiring transparency).

Can I convert specific pages only?

Yes, you can convert all pages, a specific page range (e.g., 1-5), or individual pages (e.g., 1, 3, 5) by selecting your preference in the conversion options.

Does converting PDF to image reduce quality?

Image quality depends on your selected quality level. Higher quality settings produce sharper images with larger file sizes. We offer Standard, High, and Maximum quality options.

Is it safe to convert PDFs to images online?

Yes, all conversion happens in your browser using client-side processing. Files never upload to servers, ensuring complete privacy and security.

Can I convert password-protected PDFs?

You may need to unlock password-protected PDFs first using our Unlock PDF tool before conversion, depending on the type of protection.

Is there a file size or page limit?

While there's no strict limit, very large PDFs (100+ pages) may take longer to process. For best performance, convert in batches of 20-30 pages.

Can I use the images commercially?

Yes, you can use the converted images for any purpose. However, ensure you have rights to the original PDF content.
