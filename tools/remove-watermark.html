Frequently Asked Questions

How do I remove a watermark from a PDF?

Upload your PDF file, enter the exact watermark text you want to remove (e.g., "CONFIDENTIAL"), optionally select which pages to process, and click "Remove Watermark". Download your clean PDF without the watermark. The tool uses advanced text detection to find and remove the specified watermark.

Can I remove watermarks from PDFs for free?

Yes! JustPdf's watermark removal tool is completely free with no file size limits and no registration required. However, please only remove watermarks from PDFs you own or have permission to modify. Removing copyrighted watermarks without authorization may be illegal.

Is it safe to remove watermarks from PDFs online?

Yes, absolutely safe for your privacy. All watermark removal happens client-side in your browser using PDF-lib. Your PDF files never leave your device and are not uploaded to any server. We cannot see, access, or store your documents. 100% private and secure.

Can I remove image watermarks or only text?

This tool currently focuses on text watermark removal. Image watermarks (logos, pictures) are embedded differently in PDFs and require advanced image processing techniques. For image watermark removal, you may need professional desktop PDF editing software like Adobe Acrobat Pro.

Will removing watermarks damage my PDF?

No, the tool carefully removes only the specified watermark text while preserving all other PDF content including text, images, formatting, links, and structure. Your document's integrity remains intact - only the unwanted watermark is removed.

What if I don't know the exact watermark text?

You can try common watermark terms like "CONFIDENTIAL", "DRAFT", "SAMPLE", "COPY", etc. Alternatively, open the PDF in a viewer, select the watermark text if possible, and copy it. If the watermark is an image or you can't determine the text, it may require manual PDF editing with desktop software.

Is it legal to remove watermarks from PDFs?

It depends on the situation. Removing watermarks from your own documents, draft markings, or files you have permission to modify is legal. However, removing watermarks from copyrighted content, purchased materials, or documents you don't own without authorization may violate copyright laws. Always ensure you have the right to modify any PDF before removing watermarks.

Why didn't the watermark get removed?

Possible reasons: (1) The watermark text you entered doesn't exactly match the actual text, (2) Case sensitivity is enabled but the case doesn't match, (3) The watermark is an image rather than text, (4) The watermark is embedded in the PDF as part of the background. Try toggling case sensitivity, checking the exact text, or processing all pages.

Tips for Successful Watermark Removal

  • Enter the exact watermark text as it appears (including spaces and punctuation)
  • Try disabling case sensitivity if the watermark doesn't match exactly
  • Start with "All Pages" to ensure complete removal throughout the document
  • For diagonal watermarks, the text orientation doesn't matter - just the text itself
  • If removal fails, open the PDF and try to select/copy the watermark text
  • Check the PDF after removal to ensure other content wasn't affected
  • Keep a backup of the original watermarked PDF before processing
  • Only remove watermarks from documents you legally own or have permission to modify
