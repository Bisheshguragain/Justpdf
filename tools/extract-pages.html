Tips for Best Results

  • Double-check page numbers before extracting to ensure you select the correct pages
  • Use page ranges (1-10) for consecutive pages instead of listing each page individually
  • Keep the original PDF until you verify the extracted pages are correct
  • For duplex scans, use odd/even extraction to separate front and back pages
  • Combine multiple extraction methods by using commas and ranges together
  • Use a modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, or Safari) for best performance
  • For very large PDFs, consider extracting in smaller batches
  • Remember that PDF page numbers start at 1, not 0
