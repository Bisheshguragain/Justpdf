Frequently Asked Questions

Can I edit the converted Word file?

Yes! The DOCX file is fully editable in Microsoft Word, Google Docs, LibreOffice Writer, and other word processors. You can modify text, change formatting, add content, and more.

How accurate is the conversion?

Conversion accuracy depends on PDF complexity. Simple text documents convert with 95%+ accuracy. Complex layouts with multiple columns, special fonts, or advanced graphics may require minor adjustments after conversion.

Can I convert scanned PDFs?

This tool works best with text-based PDFs. Scanned PDFs (images) require OCR (Optical Character Recognition) first. Consider using OCR software before converting, or check if we have an OCR tool available.

What's the maximum file size?

The current limit is 50MB per file. Most PDF documents are well under this limit. If you have larger files, consider splitting them first using our Split PDF tool.

Will images be included?

Yes! Embedded images are extracted from the PDF and included in the Word document at approximately the same position and size.

Can I convert password-protected PDFs?

Not directly. You must remove the password first using our Remove Password tool, then convert the unlocked PDF to Word.

How long does conversion take?

Typically 10-30 seconds depending on file size, complexity, and server load. Simple single-page PDFs convert in seconds, while large multi-page documents may take longer.

Is there a limit to how many files I can convert?

No! Convert as many PDFs to Word as you need. The service is completely free with unlimited conversions.

What if conversion fails?

Conversion may fail for corrupted PDFs, password-protected files, or files with unusual encoding. Try removing passwords, repairing the PDF, or simplifying complex layouts before converting again.
