## ğŸ“ Recommended Settings

### Font Sizes
- **Header Title**: 12-16pt (Bold)
- **Footer Info**: 9-11pt (Normal)
- **Page Numbers**: 9-10pt (Normal or Bold)

### Padding
- **Standard**: 40px
- **Formal Documents**: 50-60px
- **Casual/Modern**: 30-35px

### Background Opacity
- **Subtle**: 20-30%
- **Moderate**: 40-50%
- **Strong**: 60-70%

### Border Width
- **Subtle**: 1px
- **Standard**: 2px
- **Emphasis**: 3-4px

## ğŸ’¡ Quick Tips

1. **Match your brand**: Use company colors at 20-30% opacity
2. **Professional = Simple**: Solid border, 1-2px, neutral colors
3. **Formal = Double**: Double borders for legal/formal documents
4. **Draft = Dashed**: Use dashed borders for draft/work-in-progress
5. **Readability**: Keep background opacity below 50% for text clarity
6. **Print**: Use higher padding (50-60px) for printed documents
7. **Bold for emphasis**: Use bold for important headers, normal for details
