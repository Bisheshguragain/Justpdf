How to Merge PDF Files Online

  1. Click "Select Files" and choose multiple PDF files
  2. Arrange the files in the order you want them merged
  3. Click "Merge PDFs" to combine them
  4. Download your merged PDF file

Why Merge PDFs?

Merging PDF files is useful when you need to combine multiple documents into a single file. Common use cases include:

  • Combining scanned documents or receipts
  • Creating comprehensive reports from multiple sources
  • Consolidating chapters of a book or thesis
  • Merging contracts, invoices, or legal documents
  • Creating a portfolio or presentation from separate PDFs
